All About Stem Cells

Introduction to Stem Cells

Stem cells consist of our body's cells that will eventually differentiate to form specialized cells.

When are Stem Cells at play in our body?

Stem cells are needed in a multitude of scenarios:

  • Scenario 1: Embryonic Growth. During embryonic growth, stem cells need to rapidly proliferate to create the differentiated cells that will form the various tissue and organs in our body. /li>
  • Scenario 2: Repair and Damage. If a substantial amount of cells are lost, then the body's stem cells are needed to repair and replace that area'
  • Scenario 3: Regeneration: In some regenerating organisms, stem cells are needed to regenerate limbs and certain specialized organs.
