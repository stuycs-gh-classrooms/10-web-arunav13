Fractal Fun!

Koch Curve

Basic Curve: kotch_curve(t, 4, 15)

Modified Curve draw_kotch(leonardo, 5, 15)

  • Modification 1: Using random colors at every segment drawn by the turtle
  • Modification 2: Changing angles, such that a kotch_curve is formed through 20 degree left rotations, instead of 60 degree rotations.

Sierpinski Triangle

Basic Sierpinski Triangle: draw_sierpinski(t, 4, 100)

Modified Sierpinski: draw_sierpinski(t, 3, 5)

  • Modification 1: Instead of dividing by 2, the length of the Sierpinski will double to form the next depth.
  • Modification 2: The pensize will be twice as thick for the bottom two Sierpinskis.

Trees

Basic Tree: tree(t, 4, 15, 20)

Modified Tree: tree(t, 7, 15, 20)

  • Modification 1: The width of the tree (excluding the root) is random from 0-4
  • Modification 2: The bottom of the tree is brown, while the uppermost additions are green.
